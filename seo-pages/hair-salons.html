Everything Your Salon Needs

From the reception desk to the styling chair — Certxa streamlines every part of running your salon.

📅

Online Booking for Stylists

Clients book their preferred stylist 24/7 from your branded booking page. Block out time for color processing automatically so no slot gets double-booked.

🎨

Color Service Scheduling

Add buffer time for color processing, highlights, and treatments. Certxa automatically manages processing gaps so stylists stay productive while clients wait.

👤

Stylist Calendars

Each stylist gets their own calendar view. See who's free, who's fully booked, and move appointments between stylists with a simple drag and drop.

🔁

Recurring Appointments

Let clients lock in their regular trim every 4 or 6 weeks with one click. Recurring bookings keep your chairs full and clients loyal.

👥

Client History & Notes

Store color formulas, cut preferences, allergies, and past service history per client. Never ask twice what shade they used last time.

💰

Fast POS Checkout

Ring up services and retail products in one transaction. Accept cash, card, and split payments with tip presets and automatic receipt generation.

📊

Per-Stylist Commission Tracking

Set individual commission rates per stylist and service type. Generate accurate payout reports by date range without touching a spreadsheet.

📱

SMS & Email Reminders

Automated reminders reduce no-shows by up to 60%. Follow up after each visit with a review request to build your Google rating.

⚡

Walk-In & Queue Management

Accept walk-ins alongside bookings without chaos. Clients join a digital queue from their phone and get notified when their stylist is ready.

🧾

Thermal Receipt Printing

Print 80mm receipts directly from checkout with itemized services, products, tip, and payment method. Works with standard thermal printers.

🏢

Multi-Location Support

Manage multiple salon locations from one account. Independent hours, staff, and reporting per location — all visible from a single dashboard.

🕐

Appointment Status Tracking

Track each appointment from pending to confirmed, arrived, in-service, and completed. Keep front desk and stylists perfectly in sync.
