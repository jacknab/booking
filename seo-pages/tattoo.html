Everything a Tattoo Studio Needs

From consultation request to final payment — Certxa handles the business side so you can focus on the art.

💳

Deposit Collection

Collect non-refundable deposits when clients book online. Automatically apply the deposit to the final payment at checkout — no manual tracking.

🗣️

Consultation Scheduling

Create a dedicated consultation service type. Clients book a free 30-min design consultation — then you convert it to a full session when the design is confirmed.

👤

Artist Scheduling

Each artist manages their own calendar, availability, and day off requests. Clients book their preferred artist directly from your studio page.

📝

Client Intake Forms

Send digital intake forms for health disclosure and consent before the session. Signed forms are stored on the client profile permanently.

👥

Client History & Notes

Track each client's tattoo history, placement, style preferences, and reference photos. Make every session feel personal and informed.

💰

POS Checkout

Process final payments with deposit applied, tip included, and aftercare products added. Accept card, cash, or split payments at checkout.

📱

Appointment Reminders

Send automatic SMS reminders before the session with aftercare prep tips and studio address. Reduce no-shows and late arrivals.

🎨

Service Categories

Organize your menu by style — traditional, realism, fine line, blackwork — and by size (small, medium, full-day session). Clients know what to expect and what to budget.

📊

Artist Revenue Reports

See each artist's weekly revenue, session count, and average ticket. Pay out artists accurately with a clear breakdown of deposits and final payments.

🔁

Touch-Up Scheduling

Create a touch-up appointment type with its own booking rules and pricing. Schedule touch-ups at checkout for 8 weeks out automatically.

🏢

Multi-Artist Studios

Manage a full studio roster from one account. Each artist has their own calendar and profile visible to clients on your booking page.

🛡️

Tattoo Studio Onboarding

Sign up as a tattoo studio and get auto-created consultation, session, and touch-up service types — tailored specifically for tattoo businesses.
