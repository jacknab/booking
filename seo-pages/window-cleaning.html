Online Booking by Window Count

Clients book online by selecting window count and building type. The system auto-estimates the job price and time — no quoting calls or site visits needed for standard residential jobs.

🔁

Recurring Residential Routes

Set up monthly, quarterly, or bi-annual recurring cleans per property. Automatic reminders fill your route calendar without any rebooking work.

Commercial Account Management

Manage commercial contracts with custom service frequencies, billing contacts, and site notes. Handle interior and exterior schedules per property from one account.

On-Site Invoicing

Generate invoices from your phone when the job is complete. Clients pay by card via a text link before you pack up. No paper invoices, no chasing payments.

Client Notifications

Clients receive on-my-way texts and completion notifications automatically. Build a professional reputation with zero extra effort on your part.
