Everything a Carpet Cleaning Business Needs

From the first quote to the last room — Certxa manages every job so your team stays focused on delivering spotless results.

📅

Online Booking by Room Count

Clients book online by selecting room count and carpet type. The system auto-calculates the estimate and time needed — no quoting calls required.

🔁

Recurring Cleaning Schedules

Set up quarterly or bi-annual recurring cleaning contracts per property. Automatic reminders keep clients on schedule and your route predictably full.

📸

Before & After Photos

Capture timestamped before/after photos at each job from the technician's phone. Protect against claims and use results for marketing content.

🧪

Chemical & Equipment Tracking

Log chemicals used per job by room and square footage. Track equipment maintenance schedules to prevent costly breakdowns during peak season.

💰

Instant Invoicing

Invoices are generated on-site when the job is done. Clients pay by card via a text link before the technician leaves the property. No more paper invoices.

📱

SMS Client Updates

Clients receive on-my-way texts and job completion notifications automatically. Professional communication that builds trust and reduces complaint calls.

🗺️

Route Optimisation

Group jobs by neighbourhood and optimise your technician's daily route. Reduce windshield time, save fuel, and complete more jobs per day.

👥

Multi-Technician Dispatch

Assign jobs to specific technicians based on availability and proximity. See the full crew schedule from one dispatch board and adjust on the fly.

📊

Revenue Reports

Track revenue by technician, job type, and neighbourhood. Identify your most profitable accounts and the best times to run seasonal promotions.
