Everything a Ride Service Needs

From airport transfers to recurring medical transport — Certxa manages every ride and every passenger.

📅

Online Ride Booking

Passengers book rides online with pickup location, destination, date, and time. Confirmed bookings hit your dispatch board instantly with all trip details.

⚡

Driver Dispatch

Assign rides to available drivers with one tap. Drivers receive trip details, passenger info, and pickup address directly on their phone — no radio calls needed.

📱

Passenger Notifications

Passengers receive automatic booking confirmations, on-my-way texts with driver name, and post-ride receipts — all without any manual effort.

Recurring Trip Scheduling

Set up recurring daily or weekly trips for regular passengers (medical clients, school runs, corporate accounts). Automatic scheduling keeps your drivers fully utilised.

Invoicing & Payment

Generate invoices automatically after each trip. Passengers pay by card online or on-site. Corporate accounts get consolidated monthly invoices in one click.

Passenger Profiles

Store passenger preferences, mobility requirements, and regular trip history. Every driver knows their passenger's needs before the pickup.

Fleet & Driver Management

Manage your entire driver fleet from one account. Track driver availability, vehicle assignments, and shift schedules across your whole operation.

Corporate Account Billing

Manage corporate accounts with monthly billing cycles. Assign employees to an account and generate consolidated invoices for your business clients.

Revenue Reports

Track rides completed, revenue per driver, and trip profitability by route. Identify your most valuable accounts and optimise your fleet deployment.
