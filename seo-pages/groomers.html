Everything a Pet Groomer Needs

Pet profiles, breed pricing, appointment scheduling, and checkout — all in one place designed for groomers.

🐾

Pet Profiles

Every dog and cat gets a detailed profile: breed, age, weight, temperament, allergies, vaccination records, and grooming preferences. Always know the pet before they arrive.

📅

Online Booking

Clients book drop-off grooming appointments 24/7. They select their pet, choose the service package, and pick a drop-off window — no phone tag needed.

💲

Breed-Based Pricing

Set prices per breed and size automatically. Small dogs, large dogs, double-coated breeds — Certxa calculates the right price based on the pet booked.

🔁

Recurring Appointments

Let clients schedule their pet's regular groom every 4, 6, or 8 weeks with a single click. Recurring appointments keep your schedule full and clients loyal.

📱

Ready-for-Pickup Notifications

Send automatic texts when the pet is ready for pickup. No more phone calls interrupting your grooming flow throughout the day.

📝

Grooming Notes & History

Log coat condition, behavior, services performed, and any issues per visit. Build a complete grooming history that travels with every pet profile.

💰

POS Checkout

Ring up grooming services and retail products in one transaction. Add tips, apply discounts, and accept card or cash payments with a receipt printed in seconds.

🐶

Multi-Pet Households

One client account can have multiple pets. Book all their dogs for the same drop-off or split across the week — Certxa handles it effortlessly.

📊

Groomer Revenue Reports

Track total sessions, revenue by groomer, and top-performing services. Know exactly what's working and what to promote each month.
