Everything a Cleaning Business Needs

From one-time deep cleans to recurring weekly service — Certxa runs the admin so you focus on delivering spotless results.

📅

Online Booking

Clients book house cleaning online any time. They choose service type (standard, deep, move-in/out), frequency, and preferred time window — you get the booking instantly.

🔁

Recurring Cleaning Schedules

Set up weekly, bi-weekly, or monthly recurring cleanings per client. Automatic reminders keep clients expecting you and payments collected on schedule.

💰

Automatic Invoicing

Invoices are generated and sent automatically after each completed job. Clients pay by card via a text link — no chasing payments, no paper invoices.

Cleaning Checklists

Create custom room-by-room checklists for each service type. Cleaners mark tasks complete from their phone so clients know exactly what was done.

Client Reminders & Updates

Clients get automatic confirmation and day-before reminders. Send completion notifications when the home is done — professional communication with zero effort.

Assign jobs to individual cleaners or teams. See your whole crew's daily schedule from one board and redistribute jobs when anyone calls out.

Client Profiles & Notes

Revenue Reports

Track monthly recurring revenue, one-time jobs, and cleaner productivity. Know your most profitable service types and where to grow your client list.

Route Optimisation
