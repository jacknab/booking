Everything a Tutor Needs

From session booking to lesson notes — Certxa manages the entire student relationship so you can focus on teaching.

📅

Online Session Booking

Students and parents book tutoring sessions online 24/7. They choose the subject, session length, and available time slot — no back-and-forth emails needed.

🔁

Recurring Session Scheduling

Lock in regular weekly or bi-weekly sessions with each student. Recurring slots fill your calendar automatically without rebooking every week.

💰

Payment Collection

Collect payment at booking or send an invoice after each session. Accept card payments online — no chasing parents for cheques or cash.

📝

Session Notes & Progress

Record what was covered, homework assigned, and areas for improvement after each session. Share session summaries with parents automatically.

👤

Student Profiles

Each student gets a profile with their grade level, subjects, goals, and full session history. Stay organised across every student you teach.

📱

Automated Reminders

Parents and students get automatic session reminders by text. Reduce last-minute cancellations and no-shows without any manual follow-up.

📦

Session Packages

Sell prepaid session bundles (e.g., 10 sessions for a discount). Packages are tracked per student and credits deducted after each completed session.

📊

Earnings Reports

Track weekly earnings, sessions completed, and top subjects. See your busiest times of year and plan your availability around exam season.

🏢

Multi-Tutor Centres

Running a tutoring centre? Manage multiple tutors from one account. Each tutor has their own calendar and subject specialisations visible to students.
