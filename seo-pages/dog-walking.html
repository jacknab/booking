Everything a Dog Walker Needs

Dog Profiles

Every dog gets a detailed profile with breed, age, weight, temperament, vet info, leash preferences, and medical notes. Know every dog before you walk them.

📅

Online Booking

Clients book walks and drop-in visits 24/7. They select their dog, service type, and time window — no phone calls or back-and-forth messages needed.

🔁

Recurring Walk Schedules

Set up daily, weekday, or custom recurring walk schedules per dog. Fill your weekly calendar automatically without rebooking every single week.

📸

Post-Walk Updates

Send GPS-tracked walk reports, photos, and bathroom break updates to pet parents automatically after each walk. Build trust and get 5-star reviews.

Automatic Payments

Charge clients automatically after each walk or on a weekly basis. No more chasing payments or sending invoices — money lands in your account on schedule.

Group nearby dogs for group walks and optimise your daily route. See all walks on a map and sequence your day to minimise travel time.
