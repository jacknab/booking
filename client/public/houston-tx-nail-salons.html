Frequently Asked Questions

How do clients know which services to book?

You build a service menu in Certxa — listing each service with its name, price, duration, and description. Clients see exactly what they're booking and what it costs. No surprises at checkout.

Can I set different hours for different nail techs?

Yes. Each tech has their own schedule within the salon hours. If Maria works Tuesday through Saturday and Jenny works Monday through Friday, clients only see their actual available times.

Can I collect payment online at booking?

Yes — Certxa supports online payment at the time of booking or at checkout. You can require a deposit to hold the slot, or collect full payment upfront for specialty services.

Can I run promotions through Certxa?

Yes. You can create discount codes, package deals, and seasonal promotions that clients apply at booking. Great for first-time client specials or holiday gift packages.
